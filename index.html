The Firm
A Legacy of
Legal Excellence
"In the UAE's complex legal landscape, experience is not a credential — it is a necessity."

Al Rashidi & Partners was founded in Dubai in 1994 with a singular conviction: that every client deserves the highest standard of legal representation, regardless of the complexity of their matter.

Today, we are one of the Emirates' most respected law firms, with a proven track record across commercial litigation, corporate law, real estate, criminal defence, family law, and arbitration. Our team of 24 qualified lawyers speaks 12 languages and serves clients from 60+ nationalities.

We are registered with the Dubai Courts, Abu Dhabi Judicial Department, DIFC Courts, and ADGM Courts — giving our clients comprehensive access across all UAE jurisdictions.
